The Magical History of Disneyland

Disneyland was founded by Walt Disney and first opened its gates on July 17, 1955, in Anaheim, California. Walt Disney's dream was to create a place where families could have fun together, and that dream became a reality.

Over the years, Disneyland has grown into a worldwide phenomenon with multiple theme parks around the globe. It remains a place where dreams come true and magic is real.
